About Us

We are a group of 1st year students from USCT. While preparing for our mid and end end semester examinations, we were often faced with the problem of procuring notes from our seniors or various others sites. Making it easier for many other students like us to obtain notes conveniently was the driving force behind the creation of this resource library. One can easily navigate through the website to find notes of their current semesters which have been further dissected into respective units. Still in its developing stages, this project is a collective effort by our team comprising of Siddharth, Prapti and Avdhi.

This library is a non commercial website and aims to serve as a one stop destination to all your problems regarding engineering physics. The viewers themselves can contribute to the growth of this library by mailing us their hand written notes at our official mail.
